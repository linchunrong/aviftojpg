What is AVIF?

AVIF (AV1 Image File Format) is a modern image format that offers superior compression and quality compared to older formats. It's based on the AV1 video codec and provides excellent image quality at smaller file sizes.

What is JPG?

JPG (or JPEG) is a widely used image format known for its efficient compression of photographic images. It's supported by almost all devices and software, making it a popular choice for web images and digital photos.
